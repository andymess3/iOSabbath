iOSabbath

Forget Your Phone

Purpose.

Smartphones provide a boundless source of information, entertainment and connection, yet it's easy to become over-attached. iOSabbath is designed to give users and their phones an opportunity for a weekly day of rest.

How it Works.

Choose a day of the week from the calendar below, enter your email address and iOSabbath will send you a weekly reminder on the day you have choosen to be your day off from your phone.

In Case of Emergency.

If circumstances should arise and it becomes necessary to use your phone, by all means do. Use your phone as you normally would and return to our Sabbath when done. Happy iOSabbath.
